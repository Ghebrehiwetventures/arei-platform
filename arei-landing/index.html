The Gap

The Engine

We don't sell properties. We structure the data behind them.

Listings arrive messy — across languages, currencies, and formats. Our engine extracts, normalizes, and deduplicates every one into a single clean schema. When everything shares one structure, the market becomes readable — benchmarks, trends, and coverage that don't exist today.
