C · Kanban board

Three status columns side by side. Visual, satisfying for sweeps. But: 425 live rows don't fit a column nicely, and drag-to-change-publish-status feels too easy for a production-write.

My take

B (unified table) for the inventory surface + a dashboard strip above it. Status is one filter chip among many — search by source, island, price, etc. The needs_review queue becomes a saved filter (status=needs_review, sort=first_seen DESC) rather than its own page. The detail drawer is where the review/patch UI lives.

A is the safe, familiar option. C looks great but the data shapes don't suit a board (425 live vs 44 review is too unbalanced) and drag-to-publish is risky.
