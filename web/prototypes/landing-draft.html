Market Opportunity

Africa's real estate market is enormous — and nearly unmapped.

African real estate is one of the largest untapped data opportunities on earth. Local portals exist in some markets. A normalized, cross-source, pan-African index layer does not. That is the gap AREI fills.

Why Now

Six tailwinds that make this the right moment.

Scraping, normalization, cloud pipelines, and LLM-assisted parsing now make this infrastructure affordable to build.

Fastest urbanising continent

More city-dwellers means more transactions, more search intent, and more demand for market data.

Mobile-first internet growth

Real estate search is moving online faster than the products and data layers serving it.

Institutional capital entering

Institutional capital needs market intelligence. In many markets, there is still almost none.

AI makes parsing cheap

LLM-assisted extraction makes fragmented listing data far cheaper to normalize across languages and formats.

No incumbent to displace

Africa has no dominant pan-African incumbent. The index layer is still open.

Data network effects compound

Clean multi-market data compounds. Each added source and market strengthens the moat.

How It Works

Not a marketplace.
A data layer.

AREI sits beneath consumer products. We discover sources, ingest listings, normalize structure, and enforce trust rules before anything reaches a public surface. KazaVerde sits on top. So can APIs, reports, and third-party tools.
