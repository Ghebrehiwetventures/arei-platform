# CCore · First Broker Call Brief

**Who:** CCore — Cape Verde broker
**Prepared for:** Mikael

---

## Objective

Research call. Not a sales call. Understand how they work with international buyers. Test whether the AREI model makes sense to them.

---

## Opening

> "Thanks for the time. I'm building Africa Real Estate Index — starting with Cape Verde. The idea is to make the market easier for international buyers to understand. Your listings are already on the site. We're not a competing agency — we index public data and point buyers back to the original agency. I'm here because I want to make sure it is useful for you and accurate for buyers."

Short. Then stop and listen.

---

## Questions

Pick the ones that feel natural. Not all of them in one call.

1. How do your international buyer leads come in today?
2. Which channels bring the most serious buyers?
3. What do international buyers usually misunderstand about the Cape Verde market?
4. Where do deals usually fall through?
5. Is Cape Verde easy to compare online for someone researching from abroad?
6. Would a neutral index — not a portal, not a competitor — that sends buyer attention back to the agency be useful or just noise?
7. What would feel risky about being included in something like that?

---

## What not to say

- "We scraped your listings" → say "we index public listing data"
- Guaranteed leads
- Complete market coverage
- CRM promises

---

## Capture after the call

- Where do international buyers actually come from?
- What concerns did they raise?
- Are they open to a follow-up or pilot?
- Anything wrong with how their listings appear?

---

**Direction of travel:**
If the index is useful, the next step is simple broker tools: better listing structure, direct buyer intent, correction control, and lightweight lead handling. Not a CRM.
