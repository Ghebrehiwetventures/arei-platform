## 6. Usage notes

- Use the core descriptor consistently: *Data acquisition and intelligence infrastructure for African property markets.* Do not paraphrase it.
- Use the hero line as an opener when space allows: *Africa's property data is everywhere. We bring it together.*
- Mention Cape Verde only as Market 01 / proof market. Never imply it is the only market or the destination.
- Do not make AREI sound like a broker, listings portal, CRM, or SaaS startup.
- Do not claim transaction-price data. AREI works from listing data and applies methodology to derive signals.
- Do not claim final index accuracy before methodology v1.0 is published.
- Public copy should be simpler and calmer than investor copy. No metrics, no growth claims, no hype.
- Keep tone: clear, institutional, calm. Not promotional, not startup-breathless.
